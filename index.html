Berlin Racing Team

Since my cycling endeavour started in 2008, I became a co-founder of the local cycling team Berlin Racing Team. We started with only four riders and growed to 20 cyclists in 2012. As a web developer I am responsible for the online appearance of the team. Therefore a small Sinatra app was created to manage and serve the website. The source can be found on Github. Make sure to check the development branch.
